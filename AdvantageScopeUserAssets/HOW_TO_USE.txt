1. open advantagescope
2. click "help" in the top left
3. click "show assets folder"
4. paste the "Robot_1778" folder into the folder that is opened
5. in advantagescope, click "file"
6. click "open logs" and select the log file you would like to view
7. drag the poses into the "poses" bar in the bottom of the 3d field tab, in numerical order
    (you need to drag the poses directly onto the base pose and select that they are a component,
    if you're lost view here: https://youtu.be/unX1PsPi0VA?t=307 )
8. be sure to select our robot model by clicking on the arrow-looking thing to the left of our swerve pose
9. press space to play
